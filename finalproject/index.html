ABOUT


There once was a dog named Dakota and he dedicated his life to rescuing mine. Together, we experienced hardships such as homelessness and survived. So once he passed, it was with him in mind that I ventured to the shelter and there I found the next companion to change my life. Luka required patience, persistence, understanding, and a lot of love that I was unsure I could give. Again, Dakota was a constant reminder that Luka and I both deserved to be loved and eventually we were able to make a home.

Now I’m offering the same opportunity to others. Luka had been returned several times and struggled with high anxiety. Shortly after, another came our way. Bunni had been surrendered. That didn’t stop her from joining our adventure and giving an immense amount of love. Though I hope these dogs would have had a safe outcome regardless of my intervening, both had been left in a very dark place. Now, they sit healthy basking in the sun during the day or running free at the park. Come meet with the dogs who are up for adoption!
