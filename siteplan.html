Site Name

Dry Oar

Site Purpose with Goals

The purpose of this site is to provide a place where others may rent the material needed and necessary to enjoy white water rafting while also becoming educated of the sport. It'll provide information in regards to rental availability and requirements to ride. We want others to enjoy and love the adventure while experiencing the beauty the world has to offer. All are welcome to enjoy!

Audience

This site targets a wide variety of audiences including, though not limited to:
  • An anxious grandmother who wants to see all her granchildren enjoying themselves safely.
  • A wild adventurer at heart woman who wants to experience the most difficult rapids on the best raft.
  • The couple with young children who wants to teach them the thrills of beginners white river rafting.
  • Those looking for a vacation activity that challenges all athletes in the group.
